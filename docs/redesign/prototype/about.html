Our story

Beauty advice you
can actually trust.

We built DermaGuru because the internet is full of skincare opinions — and almost none of them are honest about what they're selling you, or where the advice ends and a doctor's begins.

Our mission

An advisor,
never a diagnosis.

DermaGuru is a skincare advisor — a calm, knowledgeable companion that helps a shopper understand their skin and find a routine they'll actually keep. It is never a medical device, and it never pretends to be one.

That single principle shapes every decision we make. The advisor can explain why a barrier feels tight in dry desert air, or how to layer a brightener without irritation. But the moment a conversation looks like it needs a professional, it stops advising and says so — plainly, in the shopper's own language.

We think trust is the only durable advantage in beauty. So we earn it the hard way: recommendations grounded in real products, safety screening that runs before anything is ever sold, and sponsored placements that are always disclosed. No dark patterns. No invented miracles.

What we believe

Three convictions, built into the product.

Not a values page that lives in a slide deck — each of these is a constraint the software actually enforces, on every single turn.

✦

Grounded in real catalog

The advisor recommends only products that genuinely exist in a brand's catalog — retrieved, never imagined. No hallucinated SKUs, no phantom ingredients, no promises a real shelf can't keep. If we can't ground it, we don't say it.

🛡

Safe by design — 2 gates

A deterministic input gate screens every message before the AI replies, and an output gate checks every response before it ships. The advisor can explain a routine, but it can never diagnose, promise a cure, or sell into a red flag.

ع

Arabic-first

Arabic isn't a translation layer bolted on at the end — it's a first-class language with native right-to-left layout, Arabic typography, and the same safety screening. Shoppers get real guidance in the language they think in.
