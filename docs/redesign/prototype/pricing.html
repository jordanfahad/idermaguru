Pricing FAQ

The honest answers.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — every Private Widget plan starts with a no-risk trial during onboarding, so you can theme the advisor and test it on your catalog before you're billed.

What counts as a consultation?

One consultation is a single shopper conversation session, no matter how many messages it contains. Reloads and follow-ups within the same session don't count twice.

Do you take a cut of sales?

No. The Private Widget is a flat subscription — keep 100% of your revenue. The public marketplace is paid by ad spend (CPC / CPM), never a commission on your orders.

Is Stripe live?

Billing runs in Stripe TEST mode during onboarding so you can complete setup without a real charge. We switch you to live billing only once you're ready to launch.

Can I run both tiers at once?

Absolutely. Many brands embed the Private Widget on-site and run sponsored campaigns in the public consultation to capture demand they don't already reach. They share one dashboard.

Is it medical advice?

Never. DermaGuru is a skincare advisor — educational beauty guidance only. A deterministic safety gate routes red flags to "see a professional" and is included on every plan.
