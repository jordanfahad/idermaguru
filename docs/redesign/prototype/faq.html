FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything shoppers and beauty brands tend to ask about DermaGuru — how the advisor works, where guidance ends, and how we keep it honest.

For shoppers

Using the advisor

Is DermaGuru a doctor or a diagnosis?

No. DermaGuru is a skincare advisor — it offers educational beauty guidance and product suggestions, never a diagnosis, prescription, or treatment. It's not a medical device, and it will never tell you what condition you have. For anything clinical, it points you to a professional.

What happens if I describe something serious?

If your message looks like a red flag — pain, bleeding, a fast-changing mole, a possible reaction — the advisor stops recommending products and refers you to see a dermatologist or doctor. That referral is deterministic: it runs before the AI ever replies, so it can't be talked around.

Are the product recommendations real?

Always. Every suggestion is grounded in a real, in-stock catalog — retrieved, never invented. The advisor won't make up a product, an ingredient, or a claim. If it can't ground a recommendation in something that genuinely exists, it simply won't offer it.

Can I use it in Arabic?

Yes — Arabic is first-class, with native right-to-left layout, Arabic typography, and the same safety screening as English. Type or speak in either language and the advisor follows along. Pricing is shown in AED.

For merchants

Running it on your brand

Can I run both tiers at once?

Yes, and many brands do. Run the Private Widget as a white-label advisor on your own store — it recommends only your products — and separately claim disclosed, safety-gated sponsored slots in the free public Live Consultation to reach shoppers already asking questions. The two work well together.

Will it ever recommend a competitor?

Not in your Private Widget. There, the advisor is grounded strictly in your curated catalog — no competitors, no noise. The public consultation is different by design: it's a shared, multi-brand surface where sponsored placements are clearly labelled.

How do sponsored placements work?

In the public consultation you can bid for sponsored slots, set budgets and caps, and we'll surface your SKUs against relevant skin questions. Every sponsored item is clearly disclosed as sponsored, stays grounded in real products, and is suppressed entirely whenever a session trips a safety red flag.

How do I add it to my store?

The widget embeds in roughly one line and themes to your brand. It supports Shopify and WooCommerce add-to-cart, with full analytics on impressions, clicks, and attributed conversions — so you can see exactly what the advisor contributes.

Safety & privacy

How we keep it honest

What is the "two-gate" safety system?

Defense-in-depth. A deterministic input gate screens every message before the AI responds, and an output gate checks every reply before it's shown. Together they ensure the advisor can explain a routine but can never diagnose, promise a cure, invent a product, or sell into a red flag.

Are sponsored results disclosed?

Always, and prominently. Sponsored placements carry a visible "Sponsored" label, and they're held to the same grounding and safety rules as everything else. Disclosure is non-negotiable — a shopper should never wonder whether advice was paid for.

How is my data handled?

Our privacy posture is aligned with the UAE's PDPL and with GDPR. We minimise what we collect, are transparent about how conversations are used, and design for regional data expectations. DermaGuru is an advisor tool — it is not a medical record system.

Does it diagnose or treat conditions?

No — and it's built so it can't. DermaGuru does not diagnose, prescribe, or treat. It offers general educational guidance and product suggestions only, and routes anything that needs clinical attention to a qualified professional.

Billing

Plans & payments

How does payment work during onboarding?

While you're getting set up, billing runs through Stripe in TEST mode — so you can configure plans, embed the widget, and trial the full flow without a real charge. We switch you to live billing only once you're ready to go.

What counts as a consultation?

A consultation is a single shopper session with the advisor — one continuous conversation about their skin, regardless of how many messages it takes. Usage and metering are based on these sessions, and they're visible in your dashboard.

What currency are plans in?

Plans and product pricing are shown in AED, in keeping with our regional focus. See the pricing page for the current Private Widget tiers and public consultation sponsorship options.

Is the public consultation really free?

For shoppers, yes — the Live Consultation is free to use. For brands, it's free to be surfaced organically; you only pay when you choose to bid for disclosed sponsored placements, with budgets and caps you control.
